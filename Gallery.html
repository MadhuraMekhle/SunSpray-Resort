About Us

For a quiet escape near Mumbai. Spread across 8.5 acres, the resort has been built to provide our customers with exclusive experiences of nature and lush greenery. Equipped with around 63 rooms, all contemporarily designed, our resort and spa are where you want to visit for a leisurely and relaxing stay. While offering you spacious rooms, modern amenities and multiple F&B outlets, our facility enables banqueting options, including poolside venues. You don't have to go too far to host classy corporate events. Our customised arrangements and niche hospitality will do the trick!
